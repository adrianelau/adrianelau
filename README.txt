# Engineering Portfolio Starter Website

## Files to upload to GitHub
Upload everything in this folder to your `yourusername.github.io` repository:

- `index.html`
- `style.css`
- `resume.pdf` once you add your resume
- the entire `images` folder

## Text to replace in `index.html`
Search for and replace:
- `YOUR NAME` / `Your Name`
- `[YEAR]`
- dates and locations for Internship and Luan Lab
- placeholder bullet points
- `your-email@example.com`
- the LinkedIn link

## How to replace pictures
1. Upload your own photos into the `images` folder in your GitHub repository.
2. Give files simple names with no spaces, such as:
   - `headshot.jpg`
   - `internship-prototype.jpg`
   - `pendulum-cad.png`
   - `robot-arena.jpg`
3. Open `index.html` and replace the placeholder image name.

Example:
```html
<img src="images/profile-placeholder.svg" alt="Portrait or featured engineering project image">
```

Change to:
```html
<img src="images/headshot.jpg" alt="Portrait of Your Name">
```

## Important
Only upload photos or company/lab material you are permitted to share publicly.
